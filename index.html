About Me

I'm currently a sophomore studying at UC Berkeley, majoring in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I've had a strong interest in technology and engineering for as long as I can remember. Some subject areas I am interested in include virtual reality (particularly of the fully immersive flavor), machine learning, and the Internet of things. I enjoy playing video games in my free time, and like any art form, games are easily capable of delivering meaningful messages to their audience. Games also possess a characteristic no other art form has: the ability to challenge the player and to immerse her in fantastic worlds that could otherwise only lie in our imaginations.
